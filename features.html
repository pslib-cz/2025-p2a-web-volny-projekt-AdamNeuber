    Interface

    Our intuitive graphical interface replaces the need to manually edit CMakeLists.txt

    Our intuitive graphical interface eliminates the need for manual CMakeLists.txt editing. SynapC synthesizes CMake, Conan, and vcpkg into a single powerful workflow, while offering granular manual control for custom libraries. Change your settings in the GUI. SynapC handles the scripts.

    Make your changes in the GUI and SynapC automatically updates your CMakeLists.txt

    Dependency visualization

    Map Your Architecture: Trace every dependency through an interactive neural graph. Spot version conflicts and circular dependencies before they break your build.

    Smart Boilerplate Injection

    Clean Code, Automatically: SynapC writes industry-standard CMake commands. No messy hacks, just pure find_package and target_link_libraries logic injected exactly where it belongs.
